Who are we?

RX Digital is an initiative to spread digital awareness by providing the necessary infrastructure to the most disadvantaged communities. This is as a response to one of the major problems these communities are facing, lack of infrastructure. Most departments representing most economic sectors have stressed a lot on the need for digitalization throughout the country but lack of infrastructure hinders this progress. Many students wish to have these resources at their disposal but such facilities are not available for them due to the fact that such communities are very much disadvantaged. This will equip learners with research skills, computer literacy and reduce the amount of time spent in the streets by spending more time in a digital learning environment. RX Digital aims to provide computer and internet access to these communities and a lot of crash courses that will improve digital awareness, performance in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Maths, Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

What we offer

internet access

We provide internet access to all parties interested in our services at a reasonable price in order to get everyone digitaly active. With unlimited wi-fi learners get to conduct researchs in a digital stimulating environment in our workspaces or computer lab, realise the value of the value of internet and take advantage of it. Members get unlimited use of our services as per subscription package.

computer Lab

This establishment provides computer access to learners and the community at large equipping learners with research skills, reduce the level of illiteracy and increasing academic performance. Most importantly, the department will contribute a lot in removing the kids and youth from the streets and replacing their daily environment with a digital development environment.

Science Lab

The associations between humans and technology, the techno-human interaction, gives rise to new modalities of behaviour, new ways of being human. This establishment will give learners practical science experiments which are usually not available due to lack of resources and more beyond the scope of the school system. A series of science labs facilitating research & experiments.

Workspace

We provide a quiet space for those who need to study, the study rooms are equipped with books, desks, computers and internet. The work space is a good environment for learners attending online classes or writing online exams. It is also very useful for conferences and individuals attending virtual meetings. Most people work online nowadays and this will increase in the near future

Projects

Educating learners in schools about forth industrial revolution so that they understand what the future holds, what skills will they need and how they will contribute to the economy. Leading project is the career simulation. Most of the careers are the ones that provide digital skills. Real life problems that certain specialists come across daily are presented to and solved by learners.

Arts and Culture

As much as digitalization is at the heart of this discussion, Arts and culture are and will always be a part of our heritage that need to be preserved. It is important to preserve arts and culture in the society and explore ways to infuse them into the digital world. Learners and society members need to be able to utilize the provided technology to put their skill sets into use and generate income with them.

Crash Courses

The roll-out of digital literacy, coding and robotics in the curriculum from foundation to the senior phase is a cornerstone in the implementation programme and needs to be supported by all stakeholders. It includes the integration of 21st century skills such as creativity, collaboration, and problem solving. We offer over 100 courses that enhance ICT knowledge & STEAMIE related subjects.

RXLC

Simulation
